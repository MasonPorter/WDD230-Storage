Colors:

The colors choosen for this site are #EA526F (A Red Shade), #353645 (A Dark Gray), #F7F7FF (A Off White), #23B5D3 (A Aqua Blue), #279AF1 (A Darker Blue).

#279AF1 will be the primary blue in use with some accenting done by #23B5D3. #EA526F will be a call to action color. The black and white will be the neutral colors for text and defaults.
