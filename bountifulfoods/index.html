Welcome to Carlsbad California!

California has somewhere between 1,100 and 1,264 miles of costal shore. while there isn't a consenus on exactly how much costal land California features, we are blessed to have a spot on it here in Carlsbad.

Carlsbad sits in a convenient location between some of the best attractions and services found in the state of California.

Carlsbad features sandy beaches, three lagoons, outstanding shopping and world-class sporting events. Carlsbad is also home to the LEGOLAND® theme park and the Flower Fields of Carlsbad Ranch, a 50 acre patch of blooming flowers every spring. Home to many world-renowned golf equipment manufacturers, Carlsbad also features the storied La Costa golf courses. There is much to see and do!
