Site Purpose:

In the year 2023, many desire to be healthier and happier. Fruits are an important way to achieve a balanced life. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), states that people need 5 servings of fruits and vegetables per day for a healthy balance. At Bountiful Foods, we strive to support healthy eating, provide organic bounty, and convenience that you need for your life.

Target Audience:

Our target audience is adults, both male and female, from age 18 to 45. Additionally, parents and mothers with younger kids. An additional type of person could be those that focus on dieting and wellness in their online searches.
